Trip Theme

History Written in Stone — Mystery Written in the Stars

NM26 blends the old and the strange: mission churches, mountain villages, cliff dwellings, lava flows, petroglyph hillsides, alien lore, desert highways, and classic roadside Americana. This portal is the home base for the route, the stays, and the story of the trip.
