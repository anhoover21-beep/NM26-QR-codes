🌄 NM26 DINING PLAN

Nights 1–8 • Balanced • No Burnout • Road-Tested

🌙 Night 1 — Tucumcari

🍔 Route 66 Kickoff

Plan: Classic diner burger & fries

Go-To: Del’s Restaurant

Backup: Kix on 66

👉 Set the tone. Easy. Nostalgic. No thinking.

🌙 Night 2 — Santa Fe

🌶️ New Mexican Experience Night

Plan: Go all-in on chile (Christmas style)

Go-To: The Shed

Backup: Tomasita’s

👉 This is THE New Mexican meal of the trip.

🌙 Night 3 — Taos

🍺 Mountain Chill Night

Plan: Relaxed food, not heavy

Go-To: Taos Mesa Brewing

Alt: Orlando’s New Mexican Cafe

👉 Kick back. Recharge.

🌙 Night 4 — Santa Fe (Return)

🫓 Fry Bread / Something Different

Plan: Indian taco / sopaipillas / lighter New Mexican

Go-To: Tia Sophia’s

Backup: Tune-Up Café

👉 Different texture, lighter feel. Keeps things fresh.

🌙 Night 5 — Socorro

🍔 Low-Key Reset Night

Plan: Easy, no pressure

Go-To: Socorro Springs Brewing Company

👉 Eat. Chill. No agenda.

🌙 Night 6 — Roswell

👽 Fun / Gimmick Night

Plan: Experience stop (not serious dinner)

Go-To: McDonald's UFO Roswell

Optional: Farley’s Food Fun & Pub

👉 Lean into the weird. Have fun with it.

🌙 Night 7 — Fort Stockton

🛏️ Survival / Recovery Night

Plan A: Drive-thru + hotel (Whataburger)

Plan B: K-Bob’s Steakhouse (if energy allows)

👉 No pressure. Match energy level.

🌙 Night 8 — San Antonio

🌉 Celebration Night (River Walk)

Plan: Walk first, choose restaurant by vibe

Go-To: Casa Rio

Upgrade: Boudro’s Texas Bistro

👉 This is your “we made it” night.
