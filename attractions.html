NM26 Field Guide

Attractions & Stops

Scenic stops, museums, landmarks, national park pages, and memorable detours along the NM26 route.

History / Hike

Bandelier National Monument

Cliff dwellings, ladders, canyon trails, and one of the core ancient-history stops of the trip.

 Landscape

White Sands National Park

White gypsum dunes, changing light, and one of the most visually distinctive stops on the route.

 Caverns

Carlsbad Caverns

Official park page for tickets, hours, bat flight info, and cavern planning. Timed entry is currently required.

 Swim / Dive

Santa Rosa Blue Hole

Quick break or longer stop for the famous blue-water swimming and dive spot right off the route.

 Roadside Weird

Tinkertown Museum

A quirky Turquoise Trail favorite packed with carved scenes, odd collections, and classic roadside energy.

 Roswell

International UFO Museum

The essential Roswell stop for exhibits, lore, and full-on alien atmosphere.

 Texas Stop

The Big Texan Steak Ranch

Historic Route 66 icon in Amarillo with the famous steak challenge and maximum road-trip energy.

 Texas Stop

Blue Bell Creameries

Brenham visitor stop with the parlor, country store, and Blue Bell experience.
