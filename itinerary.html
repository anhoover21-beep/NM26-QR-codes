NM26 Roadbook

Itinerary

Day-by-day route, stops, and highlights across the NM26 loop.

Day 1

Ponchatoula → Tucumcari

Highway Hijinks

  • Depart Ponchatoula (early AM)
  • Eiffel Tower, Paris, Texas – landmark, photo op
  • Lucy Park, Wichita Falls – picnic lunch
  • Big Texan Steak Ranch – Amarillo
  • Cadillac Ranch – Route 66 art cars
  • Arrive Tucumcari – Blue Swallow Motel, Route 66 neon
Day 2

Tucumcari → Santa Fe

Souvenir Surfing

  • Morning in Tucumcari – Route 66 signs & photos
  • Santa Rosa – Blue Hole swim & stretch break
  • Drive Turquoise Trail toward Santa Fe
  • Check-in at Santa Fe lodging
  • Easy evening near Santa Fe Plaza – dinner & early night
Day 3

High Road to Taos

Art, Adobe, Altitude

  • Depart Santa Fe via High Road to Taos
  • El Santuario de Chimayó – holy dirt & chapel
  • Truchas – artsy yards & mountain views
  • Las Trampas – San José de Gracia church & ghost signs
  • Peñasco – Sugar Nymphs Bistro (pies)
  • Arrive Taos – check-in
  • Rio Grande Gorge Bridge – walk & photos
Day 4

Enchanted Circle → Low Road back to Santa Fe

Canyon Curios

  • Depart Taos on Enchanted Circle
  • Red River – ski village, carvings, possible lift ride
  • Eagle Nest – lake views & antique shops
  • Angel Fire – Vietnam Veterans Memorial stop
  • Loop back toward Taos, then Low Road to Santa Fe
  • Rio Grande views – Pilar & roadside metal art
  • Arrive back in Santa Fe – evening in town
Day 5

Santa Fe → Socorro

Ladders & Lava

  • Depart Santa Fe
  • Bandelier National Monument – cliff dwellings & canyon
  • Valles Caldera – volcanic valley views
  • Jemez area – red rock canyon drive
  • Optional short soak or stop near Jemez Springs
  • Arrive Socorro – check-in & dinner
Day 6

Socorro → Roswell

History in Stone, Mystery in Space

  • Depart Socorro
  • Valley of Fires – lava flow boardwalk
  • Three Rivers Petroglyph Site – hillside rock art
  • White Sands National Park – dunes & sledding
  • Drive on to Roswell
  • Arrive Roswell – check-in & evening UFO vibe
Day 7

Roswell → Fort Stockton

Underground & Overhead

  • Morning in Roswell – UFO Museum / Main Street as time allows
  • Drive Roswell → Carlsbad Caverns
  • Carlsbad Caverns – main cave tour
  • Sitting Bull Falls – afternoon swim & picnic
  • Drive to Fort Stockton, Texas
  • Arrive Fort Stockton – check-in & rest
Day 8

Fort Stockton → San Antonio

Ranches to Rivers

  • Depart Fort Stockton
  • Drive through Texas Hill Country
  • Stonehenge II – roadside oddity & photos
  • Arrive San Antonio – check-in
  • San Antonio River Walk – evening stroll & dinner
  • Optional Alamo visit (evening views from outside)
Day 9

San Antonio → Home

Calling Baton Rouge

  • Depart San Antonio
  • Lockhart – Black’s BBQ (primary) or Kreuz Market (backup)
  • Brenham – Blue Bell Creamery stop
  • Final fuel / snack stop as needed
  • Arrive Home – NM26 loop complete
